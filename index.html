Research Summary

Among all the fields of artificial intelligence, my researches include machine learning methods and algorithms. Exploring the use of various ML methods for practical applications and platform in order to have intelligence environment. Lazy learner algorithms such as Case-based Reasoning and K-nearest neighborhoods or classification using frequent pattern are one of my research topics. learning the systems to do human activities with own intelligence and gain experience from what they do before is the main point in my researches.

Briefly, we retrieve similar experiences, revise it to an appropriate solution, then after the test process retains it.

The main challenge is how to retrieve the best similar experiences and how to adapt their solutions via compositional adaptation to gain the best solution to our problem.
